What is an Emoji?

Emojis are ideograms and pictographs (pictorial symbols) used to express various emotions or objects in messages. The emojis are bright and colorful and represent emotions such as anger, happiness or faces, weather, vehicles and buildings, food and drink, animals, and plants.People use emojis to express emotions like love, thanks, congratulations, or other emotions. Emoji Keyboard allows users to Copy and Paste emojis or emoticons and use it on social handles.
